Sweet'n'Sour Chicken Recipe

Description

This sweet and sour chicken recipe makes crispy fried chicken pieces with
pineapple and bell pepper served with a homemade tangy and sweet sauce.

Ingredients

  • For the sauce: water, pineapple juice from a can, white sugar, white vinegar, and orange food coloring
  • For the batter: cornstarch, self-rising flour, vegetable oil, an egg, salt and white pepper
  • Chicken: cut eight bonelles, skinless chicken breast halves into 1-inch cubes
  • Pineapple and green pepper: pineapple chunks and green bell peppers cut into 1-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Boil 1 1/2 cups of water with pineapple juice, sugar, vinegar, and orange food coloring.
  2. Make a cornstarch slurry, then mix it into the sauce until thickend.
  3. Make the batter, then stir in the chicken pieces until well-coated.
  4. Fry the chicken.
  5. Layer the peppers, pineapple, and chicken on a plate.
  6. Pour the sauce over the top.
